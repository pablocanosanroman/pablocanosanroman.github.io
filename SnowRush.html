SnowRush

Main Features

  • Made in Unity
  • Speed boost when the player lands on the floor after doing flips on the air
  • If the player lands properly on the floor, its normal will be equal to the normal of the floor tile
  • Speed boosts will add a force to the player
  • Gravity platforms that will change the gravity of the player's rigidbody
  • Use of colliders to collect coins
