The Power Of The Elements

In this page I will give details about The Power Of The Elements, a 2D metroidvania game in which the player will be controlling the one chosen by the prophecy and will have to find the lost elements in order to recover the joy and happiness in the world. Collect the elements and use their powers to overcome the obstacles of the game!

Take Coins!

Take the coins you see to increase you maximum speed, so you can get into the goal in less time!!!

Speed Boosters!

Take the speed boosters to gain an instant speed boost!!

Jump Boosts!

Get a jump boost with these platforms! Fly High!!

Gravity Changers!

You have gravity changers as well!! This one will make the player be so heavy!!

Gravity Changers!

You have gravity changers as well!! This one will make the player so light that he will go upwards!!

Do Flips in the air!

You can rotate in the air!! If you do full flips you will get an instant speed boost when you land! Do as much flips as you can to get more and more speed!!
