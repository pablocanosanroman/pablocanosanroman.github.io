The Power Of The Elements Press Kit

The Power Of The Elements is a 2D metroidvania game in which the player will be controlling the one chosen by the prophecy and will have to find the lost elements in order to recover the joy and happiness in the world. Collect the elements and use their powers to overcome the obstacles of the game!

History

I started developing games on 2020 when I came to the UK to study Computer Games Development. On my first year I learnt how to use Visual Studio using API's like OpenGL for example. On my second year I started learning game engines like Unity or Unreal Engine. This is my project for the second semester of Games Development, one of the modules of this year.

Features

  • Use Different powers to overcome the obstacles.
  • Fight enemies with the power of fire.
  • Find and collect all the powers.
